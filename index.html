What It Does
AI generates questions & one-liner facts from your notes. Delivered as nudges while you browse — only when you're idle, never when working. Learns what you know and adjusts over time.

Modes
Revision vs Study
Revision
One question at a time, passively. Triggered by idle detection.
Study Reels
Scrollable card feed you open intentionally — like Instagram Reels.
Notification Style
Casual vs Focused
Casual
Silent green dot in corner. Click when ready. No interruption.
Focused
Push notification fires after 3–4 min idle. Forced attention.
AI Agent Pipeline
How Notes Become Questions
1
Note Quality Checker
Scores notes 1–10. Supplements from web if thin (<5). Shows quality score to user.
2
Question Generator
Generates MCQs with distractors, short answers, fill-in-the-blank. Tags difficulty 1–5.
3
Fact & Flashcard Maker
Extracts one-liners, analogies, common mistakes, real-world example cards.
4
Source Finder
Finds 3–5 websites + YouTube links with timestamps for weak topics.
